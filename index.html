⚠️ The rule before you start

July 31st is the last day you're allowed to be "getting ready." August 1st you're reaching out to real businesses regardless of how ready you feel. Preparation without action is just procrastination with better lighting. Set the deadline and hold it.

Month 2 · August · First Clients
02

💡 August mindset
August will feel uncomfortable. You'll get rejections. You'll have demos that don't close. That's not failure — that's the job. Every "no" teaches you something. Every rejection makes your pitch sharper. The goal isn't perfection — it's momentum. Keep moving.
Month 3 · September · Growth
03
September — Systematize & Scale
Retention · Referrals · Systems · Growth
Growth Month
🎯
Goal: Hit 5+ clients. Build systems so growth doesn't depend on you alone.
Week 1 · Sep 1–7
Retain & Delight
  • Check in with every August client — how's it going?
  • Fix anything that needs adjusting in their setup
  • Ask for Google reviews and testimonials
  • Document client results for social proof content
Week 2 · Sep 8–14
Referral Push
  • Ask every happy client "do you know anyone else?"
  • Offer a referral incentive — 1 free month for each referral
  • Post real client results on Instagram and Threads
  • Real testimonials now replace fictional quote posts
Week 3 · Sep 15–21
Build Your System
  • Create a repeatable onboarding checklist in GHL
  • Set up GHL automation for follow-up sequences
  • Automate your own lead follow-up so nothing slips
  • Evaluate: should girlfriend become a paid team member?
Week 4 · Sep 22–30
Plan Quarter 2
  • Review what worked and what didn't — be honest
  • Set October–December targets based on real data
  • Consider raising prices for new clients to $120/mo
  • Celebrate — you built something real from $0 🌊

Master Checklist · The Non-Negotiables
Things That Cannot Slip
Every Week
  • Post content every single day Instagram + Threads — even when nothing is happening. Consistency builds trust over time.
  • Send at least 10 outreach messages per day in August Email, DM, cold call — mix it up. Volume is everything in the early days.
  • Follow up with every lead that didn't reply 80% of sales happen after the 5th follow-up. Most people give up after 1.
  • Deliver for every client within 7 days — no exceptions Your reputation is everything right now. One bad delivery kills word of mouth.
  • Document everything publicly The journey IS the content. First client, first rejection, first payment — post all of it.
  • Ask every happy client for a referral The easiest new client you'll ever get comes from someone you already served well.
  • Review your numbers every Sunday Leads contacted, demos booked, clients signed, revenue. What you measure you improve.
